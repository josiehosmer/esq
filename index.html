Professional Profile

Generalist by nature. Here for the complexity.

I started my career at a venture fund with a nonprofit educational arm, advising portfolio companies on the importance of building out a legal function — how to structure equity, protect IP, and move quickly without leaving landmines for later. I've been doing some version of that ever since.

For nearly a decade I've been one of only two lawyers on a legal team, which has made me a generalist in the truest sense. I can negotiate multimillion-dollar deals and untangle the contract filing system. I build out processes, give advice people can act on, and form the kind of cross-functional relationships that let a lean legal team actually move at the speed of the business. I'm an extremely fast learner and skilled at creative problem solving.

What I do well: I close deals, build systems that scale, and catch problems before they get expensive. I've led a strategic product divestment, run vendor onboarding and management, own global compliance, and manage junior staff and outside counsel. I even built this website.
