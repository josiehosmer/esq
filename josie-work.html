Commercial Contracting & Legal Ops
Oct 2025 – Mar 2026
Created a master reseller template for APAC use cases, including the Berca engagement, and began reviewing all templates in preparation for changes needed with new cloud products.
Consolidated and reorganized contract filing to a single location limited to current customers and partners, and sent internal communications on access and location.
Assessed Dock, Salesforce, and five identified AI/CLM vendors for contract management and database/search capabilities, and evaluated continued use of Salesforce as a contract register.
Created a high-risk contracts report for commercial and product-delivery agreements to be actively maintained and submitted to the Audit & Risk Committee.
Built and deployed Salesforce alerting for deals that fall within defined risk parameters, enabling proactive identification of high-risk contract items as they are entered.
Finalized the new Master Client Agreement and Beta Test Agreement, published both on the IR Labs website, and updated quote templates to include partner agreement links and explicit disclaimers of PO terms issued by partners and clients.
Began a broader template review program covering all standard agreements in preparation for the launch of new cloud product offerings, ensuring contract terms align with new data-processing and SaaS delivery models.
Presented on the contract template review program at the company kickoff, building internal awareness of the legal changes underway and the rationale for updated commercial terms.

Why it matters

This work built legal infrastructure the business was missing — a single source of truth for contracts, automated risk visibility in Salesforce, and templates designed to scale with new cloud products. Presenting at kickoff meant the commercial team understood the changes and why they matter, reducing friction in negotiations and ad hoc legal requests.

Strategic Transactions & Divestment
Oct 2025 – Mar 2026
Managed the Vox testing divestment workstream from end to close, standing up initial subcontracting support to enable continuity on key client deals while the permanent partnership structure was being negotiated.
Tracked tech-transfer milestones and payment obligations through to completion, confirmed the holdback payment, coordinated between IR and partner finance teams on accounting reconciliation, and established a bi-weekly cadence to manage ongoing deal pipeline and financial items.
Negotiated a permanent subcontract and reseller framework for future deals, moving from a one-off subcontract approach to a scalable go-forward partnership structure with Vox.
Drafted and progressed a deal referral agreement approved by senior leadership, addressing end-client concerns about the IR-to-partner relationship and establishing how revenue splits would work on large direct deals — moving from a 40% resell model toward a more scalable referral structure.
Supported a confidential strategic transaction process by preparing and coordinating execution of NDAs across the relevant internal teams.

Why it matters

The Vox workstream was one of the most complex active legal matters of the year — a live divestment with ongoing commercial implications, difficult counterparty dynamics, and real revenue at stake. Getting the subcontract and referral structure right directly affected the company's ability to retain and grow accounts post-divestment.

Began consolidating the supplier request form and AI use case documentation into a single streamlined intake process, reducing friction while maintaining appropriate legal oversight.
